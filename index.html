What is Prism?

Prism is an interactive platform inspired by cognitive psychology and philosophy. It does not tell you what decision to make. Instead, it helps you understand why you choose and what influences your thinking.

Psychology Behind Prism

🧠 Confirmation Bias

The tendency to focus only on information that supports our existing beliefs.

⚡ Fear of Failure

When fear prevents us from exploring opportunities and making confident choices.

👥 Social Pressure

When expectations from others influence our personal decisions.
